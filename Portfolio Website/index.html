About Me

Hello wonderful reader! Thank you for taking the time to visit my page. My name is Jake Daniels and I'm a trainee web developer currently living in Bedfordshire, England. I spent 10 years working various roles within the same IT company without ever fully settling into a role I truly enjoyed and that fulfilled me. In the summer of 2024 the company unfortunately fell victim to the uncertain econmic climate so they initiated a voluntary redundancy process. I decided to seize the opportunity and train for a completely new career that always looked appealing, but thought was never attainable. I enrolled with ITCareerSwitch and took CodeCademy's Full-Stack engineer course with the ambition to become a junior web developer upon graduating. This website is a course project to showcase all the other projects I have/will be completing on my journey.
